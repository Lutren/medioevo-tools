# Game Design Document: DUAT City RPG/Metroidvania Hybrid

## 1. Core Vision
A deep, immersive world synthesizing high-concept cosmology, complex agent-based simulation, and narrative-driven exploration. The game blends the strategic, isometric perspective of classic RPGs with the precision and world-design philosophy of a Metroidvania.

## 2. Genre & Style
- **Exploration & Navigation:**
  - **Isometric View:** Cities and key locations feature an isometric perspective for strategic overview and environmental interaction (e.g., Diablo, Baldur's Gate, Chrono Trigger, Hades, Crystalis, Zelda: A Link to the Past, Ocarina of Time, Fable).
  - **Metroidvania Structure:** Interconnected world map requiring ability-gating, exploration, and backtracking (e.g., Metroid, Castlevania, Mega Man, Dead Cells, Blasphemous, Hollow Knight, HAAK, Record of Lodoss War).
- **Thematic Tone:**
  - A blend of high-concept narrative, atmospheric tension, and world-building depth.

## 3. Inspirations
- **Thematic/Lore:** The Matrix (simulation, layered reality), The Lord of the Rings (epic scope, world-building), Supernatural (mysticism, underlying threats).

## 4. Gameplay Pillars
- **Isometric City/Location Sim:** Agent-driven interactions, environmental logic, tactical movement.
- **Metroidvania Exploration:** Ability-based progression, map discovery, environmental puzzles.
