# NARRATIVE_LENSES_v1_2

## Boundary

Narrative lenses are conceptual MEDIOEVO-original tokens. They do not copy protected authors, characters, worlds, scenes or phrases. Public output should use the internal token language, not author names.

## Implemented Lenses

- `systems_logic_lens`: causality, agents, protocols and city-machine behavior.
- `moral_conflict_lens`: guilt, responsibility, faith, punishment and choice.
- `stoic_duty_lens`: duty, inner control, discipline and acceptance.
- `surveillance_dystopia_lens`: propaganda, surveillance, obedience and language control.
- `perception_break_lens`: doubtful reality, false memory, simulacrum and paranoia.
- `mythic_archive_lens`: lineage, memory, sacred objects and source cards.
- `absurd_trial_lens`: bureaucracy, labyrinth and blocked judgement.
- `will_and_void_lens`: will, nihilism, desire and suffering.
- `knowledge_dialogue_lens`: questions, method, contradiction and definition.
- `power_knowledge_lens`: who classifies, observes and decides.

Implemented in `src/artDirection/narrativeLenses.ts`.

## Quest Hooks

RPG export v3 maps lenses to original hooks such as decode propaganda signal, judge a sealed witness, recover memory relic, verify which memory is real, escape recursive gate review and hold the gate during collapse.
