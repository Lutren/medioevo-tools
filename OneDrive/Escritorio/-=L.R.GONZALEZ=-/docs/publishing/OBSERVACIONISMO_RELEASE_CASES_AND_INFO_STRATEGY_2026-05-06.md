## Decision

Observacionismo, Observacionismo inverso, MEDIOEVO information theory, formula
work and advanced papers belong in the highest tier only as curated research.

The high tier receives proximity, synthesis and reviewed transfer maps. It does
not receive raw archives, full internal runtime, unreleased complete formula,
notebooks, private data, prompts, account access, RPG/TCG material or unbounded
consulting.

## Case Examples For Social Copy

### Case 1 - Agent With Production Permissions

Problem:

```text
An AI coding agent can do useful work, but if it can touch production without a
gate, one wrong action can damage data, infrastructure or customer operations.
```

MEDIOEVO solution:

```text
ActionGate makes the agent stop, describe the action, attach evidence, classify
risk and route destructive or external actions to review before execution.
```

Public call:

```text
Open source is where we give builders the safety layer: gates, evidence logs,
dry-runs and falsifiers.
```

### Case 2 - Team Needs Agent Process

Problem:

```text
A team wants to use agents but has no standard ficha, release checklist or
boundary between useful automation and risky publication.
```

MEDIOEVO solution:

```text
Agent Ops Pack packages the method into templates: what was done, what evidence
exists, what is blocked, and what can be released.
```

Public call:

```text
Gumroad is for time-saving artifacts. You do not buy the private engine; you buy
a cleaner way to operate.
```

### Case 3 - Synthetic Research Without False Claims

Problem:

```text
Simulations can look impressive and still not prove real-world biology,
markets, society or physics.
```

MEDIOEVO solution:

```text
DUAT Templates force a report to separate setup, evidence, allowed result,
blocked result and falsifiers.
```

Public call:

```text
We publish synthetic labs as tools for reasoning, not as prediction engines.
```

### Case 4 - Revolutionary Theory Without Leaking The Core

Problem:

```text
The strongest theory material is valuable but immature material can become
noise, legal risk or IP leakage if dumped raw.
```

MEDIOEVO solution:

```text
High-tier Sponsors receive curated theory: Observacionismo, inverse
Observacionismo, MEDIOEVO information theory, paper previews, falsifiers and
transfer maps into tooling.
```

Public call:

```text
The top tier receives intelligence curated from the research frontier. It does
not buy the raw archive or the complete private system.
```

## Release Ladder

| stage | public shape | allowed | blocked |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0. Private intake | ficha, hash, claim state | internal review | public copy |
| 1. Public seed | problem, intuition, synthetic example | low-claim post | formula/raw paper |
| 2. Tool extraction | gate, checklist, schema, demo | open-source or Gumroad candidate | private runtime |
| 3. Sponsor brief | curated monthly research | high-tier delivery | raw archive dump |
| 4. Reviewed paper | cleaned paper with falsifiers | publication candidate | unlicensed sources/data |
| 5. Product/repo | package, repo, page, talk | targeted release with ActionGate | broad workspace upload |

## Cadence

- Weekly: one public problem/solution post.
- Monthly: one public-safe build digest for low Sponsors.
- Monthly: one high-tier research brief.
- Quarterly: one reviewed paper/demo/tool extraction candidate.
- Always: private material remains private unless a target-specific gate says
  otherwise.

## Red Lines

Do not release:

- full private runtime;
- master prompts;
- complete unreleased formula;
- raw notebooks or dumps;
- private datasets/backtests;
- RPG/TCG source, mechanics, lore or assets;
- account/session/login material;
- full unpublished books or canon vault;
- claims of guaranteed safety, validated prediction, diagnosis or solved
  consciousness.
