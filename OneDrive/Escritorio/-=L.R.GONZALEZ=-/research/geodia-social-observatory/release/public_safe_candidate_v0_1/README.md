## Status

This is a local review candidate. It has not been published, uploaded, deployed, sold, or distributed externally.

## What GEODIA does

GEODIA provides a technical, offline harmonization layer for indicators from official sources and synthetic test examples. It preserves source attribution, comparability classes, caveats, and publication gates.

## What GEODIA does not do

- It does not predict votes, elections, people, or social outcomes.
- It does not rank territories, people, voters, institutions, or countries.
- It does not assert political or social causality.
- It does not represent official validation by INEGI, World Bank, Eurostat, or any source institution.
- It does not convert `STRONG_PROXY` into `EXACT`.

## Package content

This candidate includes public-facing documentation, attribution notes, a sanitized INEGI source card, QA summary, a manifest, and a synthetic fixture schema example. It does not include raw XLSX files or real fixture data.

## Review requirement

Legal and human review are required before any external distribution. The package is intentionally local-only until a separate publication gate approves release.
